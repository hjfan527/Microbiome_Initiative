Daniel Segre, PhD

Director, BU ARC on Systems Biology of Microbiomes

Professor Segre is a professor in the Department of Biology, Bioinformatics, and Biomedical Engineering at Boston University. His research focuses on the development of theoretical approaches and computational models for the study of complex biological networks, with particular interest in the dynamics and evolution of metabolism.
