Welcome to the BU Microbiome Initiative

Welcome to the Boston University Microbiome Initiative. We bring together scientists from different disciplines and departments, with a common interest in microbial ecosystems. Our research spans multiple scales and levels of complexity, with applications in global change, metabolic engineering and biomedicine. The BU Microbiome Initiative is a collaborative project focused on studying the bacterial interactions within the human microbiome.
